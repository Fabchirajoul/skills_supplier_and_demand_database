The CHIETA may collect general or personally identifiable information regarding usage and Users of the database, including without limitation, the name of the domain of each User, the IP protocol number of each User, the date and time the database was accessed, and generally any other information to establish the details of the User, usage patterns and other statistics. Users acknowledge and agree that the CHIETA may use such information in any manner reasonably related to the Supply Side Database Project.

  • POPI Act. In terms of the Protection of Personal Information Act 4 of 2013 the CHIETA may only publish the personal information of data subjects (such as students and graduates) with their consent and for the limited purpose of the Supply Side Database Project. That purpose is to create a platform for students and graduates to seek employment opportunities in the Chemical Sector through the Project. Users acknowledge and agree that they will use the personal information of students and graduates solely for the purpose of considering potential candidates for jobs, for interviews, employment opportunities and recruitment purposes, and for no other purpose. Users also undertake not to reproduce, distribute, republish or retransmit any personal information of the students or graduates for any purpose whatsoever without their consent.

  • User reports. Users agree and undertake that they will, within one month of interacting with the database, submit reports to the CHIETA electronically, with information of students or graduates that they have placed or recruited. The report must include information about the students or graduates under following headings:

    1. Student Name
    2. ID Passport Number
    3. Contracting Organisation
    4. Contract Term
    5. Contract End Month
    6. Contract End Month Year
    7. Contract Type
